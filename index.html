The best team in the world.

Vodafone Malta appointed Ms.Sheila Kavanagh as its new Chief Technology Officer, who continues to make the company grow with her hard work. Ms.Kavanagh brings with her over fifteen years’ of incredible experience in the telecoms industry and has held a good number of key positions at Vodafone Ireland since 2004, more recently in senior management positions within the Technology function. In 2012 she was appointed Head of Networks in which post she was responsible for the design, development and operation of Vodafone Ireland’s network. Ms.Kavanagh also implemented a number of transformational projects during her time in Ireland, which are the launch of HD Voice, Dual Carrier 3G, 4G and 4G advanced. The creation of these projects have all required close collaboration with other functions within Vodafone Ireland, earning Ms.Kavanagh a very broad perspective of the different business asseets of the telecoms industry.
